About Lil Peeps childhood

Lil Peep was born on the 1st of November 1996 in Allentown Pennsylvania. His parents were Liza womack (His Mother) and Karl Johan Ahr (His Father).

His Father worked as a University Professor and Mother as a school teacher. While he was a child his parents had many arguments and dissagrements with each other. This affected Gus' health in a negative way.

Soon after he was born his parents decided to move to Long Island, New York. This was a new place for Gus and he found it difficult as he struggled with socialising.

How lil peep rose to fame

During 2015 in September Lil Peep released his first album named "LIL PEEP PART 1" to soundcloud containing three songs "veins, the way i see things and praying to the sky". The release of this album reached 4000 streams.

After this he started becoming popular and began having a fanbase who loved his music. Three months later he decided to release his album "Live Forever" and had features such as yung goth and lil skil.

During 2016 september Lil Peep joins the group named Gothboy Clique
